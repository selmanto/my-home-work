Getting Started

  1. Find a simple recipe you want to try
  2. Gather all necessary equipment and ingredients
  3. Read through recipe instructions
  4. Preheat oven if needed
  5. Mix dry ingredients first
  6. Add wet ingredients
  7. Bake according to recipe instructions
